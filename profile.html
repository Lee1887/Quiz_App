THE PROFILE

Queen Elizabeth (Alexandra Mary) II.

Since 1952, Elizabeth II has reigned over 16 sovereign states known as the Commonwealth Realms. She is the Commonwealth of Nations' leader. She has been the world's oldest reigning monarch since 2015 and is currently the longest reigning head of state. Throughout her reign, she has managed to maintain the monarchy's importance in Britain. In her spare time, Queen Elizabeth II enjoys equestrian sports and attends horse races on a regular basis. She also cares deeply about dogs, particularly Corgis.
